About

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays for Juventus. He has won five Ballon d'Or awards and four European Golden Shoes during his career. The forward holds the Champions League and European Championship goalscoring records. Cristiano Ronaldo is currently captain of the Portugal national team. He was the subject of the then-most expensive transfer in association football history. He signed for Juventus from Real Madrid in a deal worth €100m (£88m) in his first season. He made his senior international debut for Portugal in 2003 at age 18. He has since earned over 170 caps and is the country's all-time top goalscorer. In 2015, he was named the best Portuguese player of all time by the Portuguese Football Federation. He is the world's highest-paid athlete and most famous athlete. He is the first footballer and the third sportsman to earn $1 billion in his career. Ronaldo has been ranked as one of the 100 most influential people in the world by Time magazine.
